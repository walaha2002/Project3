Findings and Analysis

Selecting the Best Model

The three models were evaluated based on their accuracy, precision, and efficiency. As shown above, the multiple linear regression model and gradient boosting model had relatively low accuracy and were disregarded. As shown above, the random forest and extremely randomized tree model have similar accuracy and precision. Ultimately, the extremely random trees model was selected since the modelling algorthim is more efficient and will return results faster.
